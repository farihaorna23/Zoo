Nala: The Cheetah

Nala was born in eastern range of Afria south of the Sahara Desert with her twin brother Zazo. She is not that social. She likes to keep to herself and only plays with his brother. Nala does have a mate. His name is Dal.

You can see Nala through out the whole year except when the weather gets really bad. One interesting fact about Nala is that she is extremly fast. Cheetah by nature are fast animals and are called the sprinters of the cat world.
