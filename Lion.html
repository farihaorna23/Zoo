Zuba: The Lion

Zuba is an African lion. He was born in South Africa and moved to Pawradise Zoo on 2018. Lions like Zuba enjoys being social and likes to stay together. They are called a pride. Zuba has mate named Nabila and has two cubs name Zola and Simba. Zuba is a great father to his kids.

You can see Zuba through out the whole year except when it gets really cold or it is really rainy. Zuba and his family enjoy staying indoors when the weather isn't good, so if you are planning to visit Zuba and his family, you should visit when the weather is good.
