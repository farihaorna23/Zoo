Barney: The Bear

Barney is a brown bear who was born in coastal Alaska. He loves eating salmon. When brown bears are in the wild, they usually like to catch fishes near the river. They are usually very big like barney and some even grow to become even larger.

An interesting fact about barney is that he spends four to five months a year in sleep. This is called hibernation. This helps bears like barney to stay alive during harsh winter when There is no food. So if you come during winter, you will probably see him asleep.
