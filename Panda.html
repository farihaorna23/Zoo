Bei Bei: The Panda

Bei Bei is from China. He is a very friendly one who loves to cuddle with his care giver. He also likes to roll around in the ice. Bei Bei recently became a father to two children. He loves to eat bamboo for lunch. If you are lucky, you can get to feed him. Bamboo is very critical to their diet.

He is very well suited for winter. So, if you come during cold weather you can see him enjoy the weather.
